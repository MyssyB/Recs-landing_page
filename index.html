About the Project

This patient portal provides securer access to meidcal records, test results, appointment scheduling and more. Our goal is to empower patients by making healthcare more accessible and convenient.

  • Access Medical Records:View and download your healthcare records securely.
  • Schedule Appointments:Easily book and manage your healthcare appointments.
  • Secure Messaging:Interact directly with your healthcare providers.
  • Bill Payment:Pay your medical bills online, securely and quickly
